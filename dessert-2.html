Puffy cheese and olive pastry swirls

Ingredients:

  • 1 sheet ready rolled puff pastry, thawed
  • 3 teaspoons black olive tapenade
  • 2/3 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • ground black pepper

Time:

  • Prep: 10m
  • Cook: 15
  • Ready In: 25m

Directions:

  1. Spread puff pastry with olive tapenade. Sprinkle parmesan cheese and some freshly ground black pepper. Roll pastry sheet up into a coil and cut into 1cm-wide slices. Place on a baking tray lined with baking paper and bake at 200°C for 10-15 minutes or until crisp and golden. Cool on a wire rack.
