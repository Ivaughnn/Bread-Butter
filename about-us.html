About Us

Bread & Butter

Welcome to Bread & Butter! Founded with a passion for artisanal baking and a love for creating delightful culinary experiences, Bread & Butter brings you a slice of homemade goodness in every bite. Located in the heart of San Carlos City, Pangasinan, we pride ourselves on using only the finest ingredients to craft a diverse range of breads, pastries, and confections that cater to every palate. Our commitment to quality extends beyond our products; it's embedded in our warm hospitality and dedication to customer satisfaction. Whether you're indulging in our freshly baked goods or savoring a cup of our signature coffee, we invite you to experience the essence of homemade charm at Bread & Butter. Join us on a journey where every slice tells a story of passion, flavor, and community.
